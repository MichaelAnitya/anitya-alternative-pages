Same people. Same philosophy: creation should not depend on one company's runway.

Quick answers

Is Spatial really shutting down?

Spatial announced that its creator platform sunsets on July 27, 2026. After that date, creator-built spaces are no longer accessible on Spatial. The company is moving in a different direction, and the platform its creators built on is closing.

Can I bring my Spatial spaces over?

There is no one-click import of a Spatial space, and we will not pretend otherwise. But unlike most shutdowns, your source assets are yours: if you built your environments or art in Unity or Blender, export them as GLB and import them straight into anitya, then rebuild the layout by hand or prompt the space and refine it.

Is anitya free?

Free to start. You can step into Worlds from a link without an account. Creating and publishing your own takes a free account.

Do I need a VR headset?

No. anitya is browser first. The same World link opens on desktop, phone, and VR.

Is anitya ready to use today?

Yes. anitya is live now in open beta and improving weekly. The beta tag means what it says: rough edges, actively being fixed. There is no relaunch to wait for. You can step into Worlds and build today, and arriving now means you are one of the first ones in.
