The question you are actually asking:
will this one last?

You have every right to ask.

Rec Room raised over 294 million dollars, reached 150 million players, and still could not make the costs work. AltspaceVR closed in 2023. Mozilla walked away from Hubs in 2024. If you have been burned before, skepticism is the sane response.

So we will not insult you with a promise that nothing can ever change. Here is what we can tell you instead: anitya is built by the team behind Godot, the open source engine that has outlived nearly every platform on this page and only grown stronger for over a decade. Same people. Same philosophy: creation should not depend on one company's runway.

Quick answers

Is Rec Room really shutting down?

Yes. Rec Room closed on June 1, 2026, after a decade and more than 150 million players. Logins ended, rec.net went offline, and the creator build servers closed.

Is anitya free?

Free to start. You can step into Worlds from a link without an account. Creating and publishing your own takes a free account.

Do I need a VR headset?

No. anitya is browser first. The same World link opens on desktop, phone, and VR.

Can I bring my Rec Room creations over?

There is no direct import from Rec Room, and we will not pretend otherwise. What you can do is rebuild fast: describe your room in words, refine it by hand, and import your own 3D models, for example from Blender.

Is anitya ready to use today?

Yes. anitya is live now in open beta and improving weekly. The beta tag means what it says: rough edges, actively being fixed. There is no relaunch to wait for. You can step into Worlds and build today, and arriving now means you are one of the first ones in.
