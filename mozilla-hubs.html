Quick answers

What happened to Mozilla Hubs?

Mozilla discontinued Hubs on May 31, 2024. The hosted service closed, along with Spoke scenes and the demo servers. A Community Edition exists for self-hosting, but it asks you to become a sysadmin just to keep a room online.

Can I bring my Hubs scenes over?

There is no one-click import of a Hubs or Spoke scene, and we will not pretend otherwise. But Hubs was built on glTF and so is anitya's import: your GLB models load straight in. Rebuild the room around them by hand, or prompt the space and refine it.

Is anitya free?

Free to start. You can step into Worlds from a link without an account. Creating and publishing your own takes a free account.

Do I need a VR headset?

No. anitya is browser first. The same World link opens on desktop, phone, and VR.

Is anitya ready to use today?

Yes. anitya is live now in open beta and improving weekly. The beta tag means what it says: rough edges, actively being fixed. There is no relaunch to wait for. You can step into Worlds and build today, and arriving now means you are one of the first ones in.
